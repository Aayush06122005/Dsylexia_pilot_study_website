Aptitude Test Instructions

Welcome to the Aptitude Test! This test consists of four sections designed to assess different cognitive abilities:

  • Logical Reasoning: Solve puzzles and identify patterns
  • Numerical Ability: Work with numbers and mathematical concepts
  • Verbal Ability: Understand and work with language
  • Spatial Reasoning: Visualize and manipulate spatial relationships

Time: Each section takes approximately 10-15 minutes

Instructions: Read each question carefully and select the best answer

Note: You can save your progress and return later

Logical Reasoning

1. Which number comes next in the sequence: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, ?

2. If all roses are flowers and some flowers are red, then:

Numerical Ability

1. What is 25% of 80?

2. If a train travels 120 km in 2 hours, what is its speed in km/h?

Verbal Ability

1. Choose the word that best completes the analogy: Book is to Reading as Fork is to:

2. Which word is a synonym for "Eloquent"?

Spatial Reasoning

1. If you fold a piece of paper in half twice, how many layers will you have?

2. Which shape would you get if you rotate a square 90 degrees clockwise?
